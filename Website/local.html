Local History

❮ ❯

ITB is situated in Blanchardstown.
Blanchardstown got it's name from the Blanchard family who owned an etate in the area between 1250 and 1260. For a long time it was a rural area with a small village. It shares history with it's neighbouring town until their divide around the 1960's when the first housing estates were developed.

Due to the new estates built in the 1960's the Dublin City Development Plan of 1972 decided Blanchardstown needed a town centre. Finally in 1996 Blanchardstown Shopping Centre was built. The Centre has become Ireland’s largest retail and leisure development and the country’s most popular retail park destination.
